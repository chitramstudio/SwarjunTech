Privacy Policy

ePavatiBook — receipts for NGOs, Trusts, institutions and doctors

Last updated: 8 July 2026

ePavatiBook ("the app", "we", "us") helps organisations such as NGOs, trusts, institutions and clinics create and manage receipts. This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is stored, and the choices you have. By using ePavatiBook you agree to this policy.

1. Information we handle

  • Account information — if you sign in, we use your email address (email & password sign‑in) or your Google account name and email (Google Sign‑In) to create and secure your account.
  • Organisation profile — organisation name, type, address, phone, email and receipt numbering settings that you enter. Your uploaded organisation logo is stored on your device only and is never uploaded.
  • Receipt & contact data — details you enter to issue receipts, such as the payer's name, mobile number, address, place, amount, receipt type, notes and dates.

We do not collect location, contacts, or usage‑tracking analytics.

2. How your data is stored

  • On your device — all data is stored locally on your device so the app works fully offline.
  • In the cloud (only if you sign in) — your organisation profile and receipts are backed up and synced to Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication) so you can restore them and use multiple devices.
  • Local / guest mode — if you use the app without signing in, your data stays on your device and is never uploaded.

3. How we use your data

Your information is used solely to provide the app's features: creating and storing receipts, generating PDFs, and (for signed‑in users) backing up and syncing your data across your devices. We do not sell your data.

4. Advertising

The free (Normal) version of ePavatiBook shows banner advertisements supplied by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may collect and use device information (such as an advertising identifier) in accordance with Google's advertising policies. Ads are not shown to Prime (paid) accounts. We do not share your receipt or organisation data with advertisers.

5. Service providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to authenticate accounts and store synced data, and Google AdMob to show ads in the free version. Their handling of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Access to your synced data is restricted by security rules so that only your signed‑in account can read or write it.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your synced data for as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and its cloud data at any time:

  • In the app: open the Account tab and tap Delete account.
  • Or see our account deletion page, or email us at swarjuntech@outlook.com.

Deleting your account removes your organisation profile and receipts from the cloud and closes your sign‑in. Data saved only on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

7. Security

Data in transit is protected using encryption (HTTPS/TLS), and cloud data is protected by per‑account security rules. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Children's privacy

ePavatiBook is intended for organisations and business use and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email swarjuntech@outlook.com.
