Privacy Policy

Lekha Invoice — GST & Tax Invoice Generator

Last updated: July 14, 2026  |  Effective: July 14, 2026  |  Developer: Swarjun Tech  |  Contact: lekhainvoice@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Swarjun Tech ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses and protects information when you use the Lekha Invoice application ("App").

Please note: Cloud features in Lekha Invoice are provided by Google Firebase. If you create an account and hold an active Prime plan, your invoices are uploaded to a Firebase (Cloud Firestore) database that we operate, so they can be synced across your devices. Without an account, the App does not upload your business data at all.
Summary: The App works fully offline and stores everything on your device. Nothing leaves your device unless you create an account. Even then, only your invoices, your device list and your business/organization profile are uploaded — your customer list, your inventory and your shop logo always stay local. We never sell your data.

1. Using the App Without an Account

You can install and use Lekha Invoice without signing in. In this offline mode, all business profiles, inventory items, customers, and generated invoices are stored locally on your device in an on-device database (Isar). We do not receive this data.

Even in offline mode the App may still send crash reports and, on supported platforms, request advertisements. See sections 5 and 6.

2. Accounts and Cloud Sync (Optional)

Cloud sync is optional. It requires you to create an account and to hold an active Prime (paid) plan. Signing in alone does not upload anything; sync only runs while your Prime plan is active.

2.1 Account information

We use Firebase Authentication (email and password). We store your email address, and Firebase stores your password in hashed form. We never see your password in plain text.

2.2 What is uploaded to our cloud database

When your account is signed in and Prime is active, the following is stored in Cloud Firestore under your account:

DataWhat it contains
Invoices Invoice number, date, line items (product name, quantity, unit, rate, discounts, taxes), totals and amount paid. Each invoice also carries a snapshot of the details printed on it — the customer's name, phone, address and tax number, and your shop's name, phone, email, address, tax number and terms.
Device registry For each device signed in to your account: a random device identifier generated by the App, the device name you set, the platform (Android / iOS / Windows / macOS / Linux), whether it is the master device, and registration / last-seen timestamps.
Account & plan Your Prime status and validity dates, plan limits (maximum devices, master devices and invoices), and counts of devices and invoices.
Organization / business profile Organization name, contact number and email, plus the shop details your master device shares with your other devices.
Please note: although your customer list and inventory are never uploaded as such, an invoice necessarily contains the customer details printed on it. If you sync invoices, those customer details are uploaded as part of the invoice.

2.3 What is never uploaded

  • Your customer records (the customer list itself).
  • Your inventory / product records.
  • Your shop logo image — it stays on the device.
  • Your photos, contacts, files, or location.

2.4 Who can access it

Your cloud data is stored in a Firebase project operated by Swarjun Tech on Google Cloud infrastructure. Security rules restrict access so that only your own signed-in account can read or write your data — other users cannot. A small number of authorised Swarjun Tech administrators can technically access the database through the Firebase console for support, security and maintenance. We do not sell, rent or share your business data with third parties for their own purposes.

3. Devices and the Master Device

Each account has a limited number of devices, exactly one of which is the master device. The master device aggregates invoices from your other devices, owns the shared organization profile, can remove other devices, and is the only device that can delete the account. Other devices upload their own invoices but do not read back other devices' data.

4. Media and Camera

The App requests access to your gallery or camera solely so you can attach a shop logo to your invoice templates. The image is stored locally and is not uploaded. We do not access, scan or upload your other photos.

5. Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes and errors (on Android, iOS and macOS). When the App crashes, Crashlytics may collect diagnostic information such as your device model, operating system version, app version and configuration, the time of the crash, a stack trace, and a Crashlytics-generated installation identifier. Crash reports do not contain your invoices, customers or inventory.

6. Advertising

The App may display banner advertisements through Google AdMob. Ads are shown only when we have enabled them remotely and your account is not an active Prime account — active Prime users see no ads.

To serve ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and process data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, device information and ad interactions, and may use cookies or similar technologies. This processing is governed by Google's own privacy policy. You can limit ad personalisation in your device settings (for example, "Delete/Reset advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android, or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).

7. Payments

We do not collect or store your payment card details in the App. Where a Prime plan is purchased, payment is handled by the applicable payment provider (for example, Google Play). We receive only confirmation of your subscription status, which is reflected as the Prime flag and validity dates on your account.

8. Legal Basis and How We Use Data

We use the information described above only to:

  • authenticate you and keep your account secure;
  • sync your invoices between the devices on your account;
  • enforce your plan's device and invoice limits;
  • diagnose crashes and improve the App's stability;
  • display advertisements to non-Prime users;
  • provide customer support and meet legal obligations.

We do not use your invoice, customer or inventory data for advertising, for profiling, or to train machine-learning models.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Cloud data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all of its cloud data at any time from within the App, on your master device:

Settings → Cloud & Sync Identity → Delete Account

This permanently deletes your sign-in credentials, all invoices stored in the cloud, your device registry and your account record. It cannot be undone. You may also choose, in the same dialog, to erase the data held locally on that device. Data on your other devices remains on those devices until you erase or uninstall the App there.

Full details, including how to request deletion by email if you no longer have access to your master device, are on our Account & Data Deletion page.

Crash reports held by Firebase Crashlytics are retained for up to 90 days and are not linked to your invoice data.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to withdraw consent. You can exercise most of these rights directly in the App — your data is visible and editable in the App, and account deletion is built in. For anything else, contact us at lekhainvoice@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

12. Security

Data in transit is encrypted with TLS, and data at rest in Firebase is encrypted by Google. Access to your cloud data is restricted by server-side security rules to your own authenticated account. Nevertheless, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. International Transfers

Firebase stores and processes data on Google Cloud infrastructure, which may be located outside your country. By using cloud sync, you consent to this transfer and processing.

14. Children's Privacy

The App is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.
