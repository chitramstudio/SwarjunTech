Delete Your DhanLekha Account & Data

This page explains how to request deletion of your DhanLekha account (published by [YOUR DEVELOPER NAME]) and the associated data.

Delete from within the app (fastest)

  1. Open DhanLekha and sign in.
  2. Go to the Account tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete.

If you see a “please log in again” message, sign out and back in, then retry (this is a security requirement for account deletion).

Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email [YOUR SUPPORT EMAIL] from the email address on your account with the subject “Delete my account.” We will verify and process the request.

What is deleted

  • Your login/account credentials (email and password).
  • Your profile: name, email address, and phone number.
  • Your public lookup entry (name and phone search keys).
  • Your push-notification token.

What is kept, and why

  • Shared expense/ledger records that also belong to the friends or groups you split with are retained so those other users keep their own records. After your account is deleted, these no longer contain your name or contact details.
  • Data stored only on your phone (your personal transaction history in the app) is removed when you uninstall the app; it is never stored on our servers.

Delete specific data without deleting your account

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove individual records. Inside the app you can delete:

  • Individual transactions.
  • Friend ledgers.
  • Groups and their split expenses.

Deleting a shared item also removes it for the other members it was shared with.

Timing

Account and profile data are removed from our active systems upon request. Residual copies in routine backups are purged within 90 days.
