This page explains how to delete your Lekha Invoice account and the data associated with it. It supplements our Privacy Policy.

No account? Nothing to delete. If you have never signed in, Lekha Invoice keeps all of your data on your device only. Uninstalling the App removes it. We hold nothing.

1. Delete Your Account from Inside the App

Account deletion is performed on your master device — the device that aggregates invoices for your account. This is a safety measure: it prevents a secondary or shared device from erasing your whole business account.

  1. Open Lekha Invoice on your master device.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the Cloud & Sync Identity section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by entering your account password and typing DELETE.
  6. Optionally tick "Also erase all data on THIS device" if you want the local invoices, customers and inventory on that device wiped as well.
  7. Tap Delete account.
This is permanent and cannot be undone. Your invoices in the cloud are erased immediately. Export or print anything you need to keep before you delete.

2. What Gets Deleted

Data Deleted? Details
Sign-in credentials (email & password) Yes — immediately Your Firebase Authentication user is deleted. The email becomes free to register again.
Invoices stored in the cloud Yes — immediately Every invoice synced from every one of your devices, including the customer and shop details recorded on them.
Device registry Yes — immediately All registered devices, their names, platforms and last-seen timestamps.
Account & plan record Yes — immediately Prime status and dates, plan limits, counters, and your organization / business profile.
Data on the device you delete from Your choice Erased only if you tick the "Also erase all data on THIS device" option. Otherwise it stays, and the device simply returns to offline-only use.
Data on your other devices Not automatically Those devices keep their local copies and stop syncing. Uninstall the App on each device, or use its own local data-clearing option, to remove them.
Crash reports Retained up to 90 days Firebase Crashlytics diagnostics (device model, OS version, stack traces). They contain no invoice, customer or inventory content.
Records we must keep by law Retained as required For example, payment or tax records relating to a Prime purchase, kept only for the period the law requires.

3. If You Cannot Use the App

If you have lost access to your master device, cannot sign in, or have already uninstalled the App, you can ask us to delete your account for you.

Email lekhainvoice@gmail.com from the email address registered to the account, with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Please include the registered email address. We may ask you a question or two to confirm the request genuinely comes from the account owner, so that nobody else can delete your business records.

We will action verified requests within 30 days and confirm by email once the account and its cloud data have been deleted.

4. Deleting Local Data Only

If you want to clear the data on a device but keep your account, you do not need to delete the account at all — uninstall the App on that device, or clear its storage from your operating system's app settings. On the master device you can also remove other devices from the account under Settings → Cloud & Sync Identity → Manage Devices, which frees their device slots without deleting any account data.
