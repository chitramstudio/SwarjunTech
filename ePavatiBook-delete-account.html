Delete Your Account

ePavatiBook — receipts for NGOs, institutions and doctors

Last updated: 8 July 2026

You can permanently delete your ePavatiBook account and the data associated with it at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open ePavatiBook and make sure you are signed in.
  2. Go to the Account tab.
  3. Scroll to Legal & support and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and cloud data are deleted immediately.

If you are asked to sign in again for security, sign out and sign back in, then repeat the steps above.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email swarjuntech@outlook.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject "Delete my ePavatiBook account". We will verify your request and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account sign‑in (Firebase Authentication).
  • Your organisation profile stored in the cloud.
  • All receipts and contact details synced to the cloud.

Receipts saved only on your device (for example, data created in local / guest mode, or before signing in) are removed when you uninstall the app. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Questions

Contact us at swarjuntech@outlook.com or read our Privacy Policy.
