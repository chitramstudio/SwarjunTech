Saviour

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Saviour ("the App", "we", "us"), a mobile game developed by Harshal Subhash Kakade (an individual developer), handles information when you play. We built Saviour to be played without accounts or sign-ups, and we do not ask you for personal details. Please read this policy to understand what limited data is involved and why.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, or account information, and Saviour has no login. The only data involved is:

  • Data collected automatically by our advertising partner. Saviour shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier, approximate location (derived from IP address), device type and settings, and app-interaction/ad-performance data. This collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy (see Section 4).
  • Data stored only on your device. Your game progress (unlocked levels, best completion times) and your preferences (such as the vibration setting) are saved locally on your device. This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you use "Reset progress" in Settings or uninstall the App.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display and, where applicable, personalize in-app advertisements.
  • To remember your progress and preferences between sessions.
  • To keep the App working correctly and to prevent abuse/fraud in ad delivery.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Advertising

Saviour is free and supported by ads (banner and full-screen "interstitial" ads) delivered by Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, these ads may be personalized (based on your advertising identifier and interests) or non-personalized.

  • European Economic Area, UK & Switzerland: where required, you will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, and you can change your choice at any time.
  • Controlling ad tracking: you can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings — on Android via Settings → Privacy → Ads, and on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on Google AdMob to provide advertising. Google's handling of data is described here: policies.google.com/privacy, and how Google uses information from apps that use its services is described here: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

5. Data Retention

We do not store your data on our own servers. On-device progress and preferences remain until you reset them or uninstall the App. Data handled by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's policies.

6. Security

Because we don't collect or store personal information on our own servers, the risk surface is small. Data saved on your device is protected by your device's own security. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Saviour is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided such information, please contact us so we can address it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, and to opt out of personalized advertising or the "sale"/"sharing" of information. Because the data involved is handled largely by Google AdMob, you can exercise many of these choices through your device's ad settings (Section 3) and Google's controls. You may also contact us using the details below.

9. International Users

Saviour is available in multiple regions. By using the App, you understand that advertising data may be processed by Google in countries other than your own, in accordance with Google's policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes may also be noted within the App.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how the App handles data, contact Harshal Subhash Kakade at swarjuntech@outlook.com.
